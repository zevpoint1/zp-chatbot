PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
-------------------
The Zevpoint Aveo 3.6 Plus is a smart portable AC electric vehicle charger with 3.6 kilowatt power output. This is the only 3.6 kW charger in the market that offers Manual control via touch buttons along with smart charging features used with WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity. The Aveo 3.6 Plus features a mobile app for monitoring and controlling charging sessions, making it ideal for both home charging and travel. The charger includes IP66 rating for enhanced durability, a wall mounting bracket, and a carry bag for portability.

SMART CONNECTIVITY FEATURES
---------------------------
The Aveo 3.6 Plus is a smart connected charger with the following connectivity capabilities:

WiFi Connectivity: Built-in WiFi allows remote monitoring and control of charging sessions
Bluetooth Connectivity: Local Bluetooth connection for device pairing and setup
Mobile App Integration: Compatible with Zevpoint Home app available on both Apple App Store and Google Play Store

Smart App Features and Capabilities:
- Remote control of charging sessions from your smartphone
- Real-time monitoring of charging status and power consumption
- Control charging speed from 8A (1.6kW) to 16A (3.6kW)
- Schedule charging to start and stop at specific times
- View complete charging history and records
- Track energy usage and charging statistics
- Control charging parameters remotely

App Setup Process: Download the Zevpoint Home app from Apple Store or Play Store, complete registration or sign in to your account, enable Bluetooth and WiFi on your mobile device, and connect the app to your Aveo 3.6 Plus charger.

INSTALLATION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
To safely install and operate the Aveo 3.6 Plus, the following requirements must be met:

Power Socket: A dedicated 16 ampere electrical socket is required. This socket must have sufficient connected load capacity to handle the charger's power requirements.

Electrical Wiring: The wiring must use appropriate cable sizing based on local electrical codes. Minimum 4 square millimeter cable is recommended for safe current carrying capacity.

Earthing System: Proper earthing (grounding) is mandatory for safe operation. The earthing must comply with local electrical safety codes and standards.

Protection Devices: Protection devices are required for installation:

- Surge Protection Device (SPD) 270V 20kA-40kA to protect against voltage spikes and surges

Network Requirements: For smart features, WiFi network connectivity is required at the installation location. The charger should be within range of your WiFi router for app connectivity.

Installation Options: The Aveo 3.6 Plus can be wall-mounted using the included bracket for permanent home installation, or kept portable using the carry bag for travel use.

PRODUCT COMPARISON WITH AVEO 3.6
---------------------------------
The Aveo 3.6 Plus is an upgraded version of the base Aveo 3.6 model with the following key differences:

Smart Connectivity: The Plus model includes WiFi and Bluetooth with app control, while the base Aveo 3.6 does not have smart features.
Cable Length: Aveo 3.6 Plus has 6 meter cable versus 5 meter cable on base model.
Current Adjustment: Aveo 3.6 Plus offers flexible current adjustment to any level from 8A to 16A, while base Aveo 3.6 has fixed switchable settings at 8A, 10A, and 16A.
Included Accessories: Plus model includes wall bracket and carry bag as standard.
Price: Aveo 3.6 Plus is priced at Rs. 17,999
