

PRODUCT IDENTIFICATION
----------------------
Product Name: Aveo 3.6
Manufacturer: Zevpoint
Category: Portable AC EV Charger
Power Rating: 3.6 kilowatts
Model Variant: Base model 

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
-------------------
The Zevpoint Aveo 3.6 is a portable AC electric vehicle charger with 3.6 kW power output. This charger features a metallic finish and is designed for single-phase charging applications with Type 2 connector compatibility.

ELECTRICAL SPECIFICATIONS
--------------------------
The Aveo 3.6 charger operates with the following electrical parameters:

Power Output: 3.6 kilowatts
Current Options: The charger has switchable current settings at 8 amperes, 10 amperes, or 16 amperes
Input Voltage: Accepts 220 to 240 volts AC
Phase Configuration: Single phase electrical system
Connector Standard: Type 2 connector following IEC 62196 standard
Plug Type: 16 ampere rated 3-pin plug
Charging Cable: 5 meter length cable included

PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS
-------------------------
The Aveo 3.6 has the following physical dimensions and properties:

Size: 250 millimeters length by 120 millimeters width by 80 millimeters height
Weight: 2.5 kilograms total weight
Color: Metallic finish
Environmental Protection: IP66 rating for both water resistance and dust resistance

SAFETY AND PROTECTION FEATURES
-------------------------------
The Aveo 3.6 includes multiple safety mechanisms:

Thermal Protection: Overheat prevention system with temperature monitoring prevents damage from excessive heat buildup during extended charging sessions
Electrical Protection: Short circuit protection with instant automatic disconnection in fault conditions
Overcurrent Protection: Prevents damage from excessive current draw beyond rated capacity
Overvoltage Protection: Guards against input voltage spikes and power surges from the electrical grid
Undervoltage Protection: Prevents operation when input voltage drops below safe levels
Ground Fault Protection: Proper grounding support with continuous earth monitoring ensures electrical safety
Leakage Protection: Residual current detection prevents electric shock hazards
Weather Protection: IP66 water immersion protection allows operation in heavy rain and wet conditions
Environmental Protection: IP66 complete dust sealing prevents particle ingress in dusty environments

INSTALLATION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
To safely install and operate the Aveo 3.6, the following requirements must be met:

Power Socket: A dedicated 16 ampere electrical socket is required. This socket must have sufficient connected load capacity to handle the charger's power requirements.

Electrical Wiring: The wiring must use a minimum cable size of 4 square millimeters. This ensures safe current carrying capacity.

Earthing System: Proper earthing (grounding) is mandatory for safe operation. The earthing must comply with local electrical safety codes.

Protection Devices: Two protection devices are required for installation:
- Miniature Current Breaker (MCB) of 20A 2 pole
- Surge Protection Device (SPD) 270V 20kA-40kA to protect against power surges
