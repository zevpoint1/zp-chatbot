INSTALLATION REQUIREMENTS BY CHARGER
------------------------------------

3.6 kW CHARGERS (Aveo 3.6, Aveo 3.6 Plus):
- Power Supply: Single-phase 220-240V
- Socket: 16A dedicated socket
- Cable: 4 sq mm, 3-core copper cable
- MCB: 20A double-pole
- Earthing: Required

7.5 kW CHARGERS (Dash, Dash AIO, Aveo Pro, Polar Pro):
- Power Supply: Single-phase 220-240V
- Socket: 32A dedicated socket (CEE socket included with portable chargers)
- Cable: 6 sq mm, 3-core copper cable (10 sq mm in hot climates)
- MCB: 40A double-pole
- SPD: Required
- Earthing: Required

11 kW CHARGERS (Aveo X1, Polar X1):
- Power Supply: Three-phase 400-440V (required for full 11kW)
- Socket: 16A 5-pin socket (included with Aveo X1)
- Cable: 6 sq mm, 5-core copper cable (10 sq mm in hot climates)
- MCB: 25A four-pole
- SPD: Required
- Earthing: Required

22 kW CHARGERS (Polar Max):
- Power Supply: Three-phase 400V (mandatory)
- Cable: 6 sq mm, 5-core copper cable (10 sq mm in hot climates)
- MCB: 40A four-pole
- SPD: Required
- RCD: Required
- Earthing: Required

SPYDER AIO (Up to 16kW):
- Single-phase mode (up to 7.2kW): 6 sq mm, 3-core copper cable
- Three-phase mode (up to 16kW): 6 sq mm, 5-core copper cable
- Socket: 32A CEE socket (included for single and three-phase)
- RCB and SPD: Required
- Earthing: Required

GENERAL INSTALLATION NOTES
--------------------------
- All installations require proper earthing
- Voltage stabilizer recommended if location has frequent power fluctuations
- In hot climate regions, upgrade to 10 sq mm cable for safety
- Cable length from charger: 5-6 meters typically
