UNIQUE DUAL-CONNECTOR SYSTEM
-----------------------------
The Dash AIO's revolutionary dual-connector system provides exceptional versatility:

Two Connector Types Included:
The Dash AIO comes with two different power connectors that can be swapped based on your charging location and available power socket type:

16 Ampere Type M 3-Pin Standard Plug:
This is the standard Indian household plug configuration (Type M, also known as South African plug type). It works with regular 16 ampere household electrical sockets found in virtually every home, office, and building in India. This plug delivers up to 3.6 kilowatt charging power with adjustable current from 8A to 16A. This connector is ideal for overnight charging at home, charging at friend's or relative's homes, hotel rooms during travel, or any location with standard household electrical outlets.

32 Ampere CEE Industrial Plug:
This is a professional-grade industrial plug configuration designed for high-power applications. It works with dedicated 32 ampere industrial sockets and delivers the full 7.5 kilowatt fast charging power with adjustable current from 8A to 32A. This connector is ideal for dedicated home charging installations with proper wiring, workplace charging locations with industrial power, service centers or commercial facilities, and any location with robust electrical infrastructure.

How the Dual-Connector System Works:
The Dash AIO has a modular plug interface where either connector can be attached to the main charger body. Users simply swap connectors based on the available power socket at their charging location. When the 16A plug is connected, the charger automatically limits maximum current to 16A to protect the household socket. When the 32A plug is connected, the charger allows up to 32A current for full 7.5 kW fast charging.

Connector Swapping:
Swapping between connectors is quick and simple. Disconnect charger from power socket, press release mechanism on current connector, remove connector from charger body, align and insert desired connector, push firmly until it clicks into place, verify secure connection, and connect to appropriate power socket. The entire swap process takes less than 30 seconds and requires no tools.

Why Dual-Connector is Revolutionary:
Traditional portable chargers include only one plug type, meaning users must purchase separate chargers or expensive adapter accessories to work with different socket types. The Dash AIO eliminates this problem by including both plug types in one package. This is particularly valuable in India where homes may have standard 16A sockets while workplaces may have industrial 32A sockets. With Dash AIO, one charger handles all scenarios.

SPD: Surge Protection Device recommended
RCB: Residual Current Breaker recommended

CONNECTOR SWAPPING DETAILED GUIDE
----------------------------------
Step-by-step instructions for swapping between 16A and 32A connectors:

Before Swapping:
Ensure charger is disconnected from power socket and not actively charging. Allow charger to cool down if it was recently used for extended charging session. Have both connectors available (stored in carry bag when not in use).

Removing Current Connector:
Locate the connector release mechanism on the charger body where plug attaches. Press or turn the release mechanism according to charger design (consult user manual for specific model). While holding release, gently pull connector straight out from charger body. Do not twist or bend connector during removal. Store removed connector in carry bag for protection.

Installing Desired Connector:
Select the connector appropriate for your target power socket (16A for household, 32A for industrial). Align connector pins with charger body socket. Ensure proper orientation (connector will only fit one way). Push connector firmly straight into charger body. You should hear or feel a click when connector is fully seated and locked. Gently tug connector to verify it is securely locked in place.

After Installation:
Inspect connection point to ensure good fit with no gaps. Check that connector pins are clean and undamaged. Plug charger into appropriate power socket. Verify on display that charger recognizes connector type. Begin charging as normal.

Important Safety Notes:
Never swap connectors while charger is plugged into power socket or while actively charging. Never force connector if it doesn't fit easily - check orientation. Always verify secure locking before using charger. Keep unused connector clean and dry in carry bag.

CURRENT ADJUSTMENT OPERATION
-----------------------------
The Dash AIO features manual current adjustment:

Available Current Settings:
With 32A Plug: Typically offers switchable current settings from 6A to 32A, often at intervals like 6A,8A, 10A, 13A, 16A, 20A, 24A, 28A, 32A.
With 16A Plug: Current is automatically limited to maximum 16A. Settings typically 6A, 10A, 13A, 16A.

How to Adjust Current:
Current adjustment is controlled via physical buttons or switches on the charger unit. The current setting is displayed on the integrated LED screen. Users can change current setting before starting a charging session or during charging if needed.

When to Reduce Current:
Use lower current settings when home electrical circuit cannot support full current safely, other high-power appliances operating simultaneously (air conditioning, water heaters, cooking appliances), socket capacity is uncertain or socket is older, experiencing circuit breaker trips at higher current, voltage drops noticeably under load (lights dimming), or electrical wiring quality is uncertain.
